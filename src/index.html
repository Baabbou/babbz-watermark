Watermarker

Watermarking is the process of adding text, logos, or patterns to an image to indicate ownership, provide protection, or enhance branding. It's widely used to deter unauthorized use of images and ensure attribution.

How to Add a Watermark Using This Form

  1. Select an Image: Use the "Choose File" button to upload an image in one of the supported formats: .jpg, .jpeg, or .png.
  2. Enter Your Watermark Text: In the "Watermark" input field, type the text you wish to overlay on the image.
  3. Submit: Click the "Add a watermark" button to apply the watermark. The process will overlay the entered text onto the uploaded image, ensuring your content is protected.
